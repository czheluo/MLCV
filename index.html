About Me


I am currently a master student in laboratory of wheat genetics and molecular breeding in Yangzhou University. Motivation (future): since I was little kid who always want to be a doctor and could save many people from the illness or pain. Nevertheless, clearly, I miss the chance, which fail to choice the right major. However, when I get to the area that included biostatistics and bioinformatics, which I know I still have the chance. We also know that a doctor (clinician) one day may save the most 10 or less after so many hard work. But as a scientist, I work out the research behind the disease and I can help far more people – possibly 10 million, or even 10 billion.
